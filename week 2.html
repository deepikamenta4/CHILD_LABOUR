Child Labor Awareness – WEEK 2 (01-07-25 to 07-07-25)

Objective of the Activity Done

In the second week we created awareness in provision stores and roadside shops. We also taught about the importance of education for children and the impacts of child labour.

Detailed Report

DAY 1

  • Continued our awareness program in the area and the people received it willingly.
  • Gave suggestions about the present situation on child labour.
  • Explained the impacts of child labour.
  • Explained the importance of education.
  • Gathered information about children going to schools and those working in fields.

DAY 2

A survey conducted in provision stores revealed many children are employed. Causes include poverty, lack of education, and exploitation. Recommendations include enforcing child labour laws, promoting education, and supporting families.

  • Questioned people about child labour near them.
  • Asked if they know about child rights and gathered their responses.

DAY 3

Created awareness in provision stores by explaining child rights and freedom in society.

DAY 4

Started a survey on roadside shops to check if children are working there.

DAY 5

Created awareness among people in roadside shops, explaining child rights and the importance of education for children.
